FAQ

 How long does a repair take?

Repairs are usually completed same-day but may take 1-2 days depending on volume and part availability.

Do you offer any warranty on repairs?

If you're not satisfied with a repair within 30 days, bring the device back. We'll re-examine it at no charge and either fix the issue or provide a refund — no hassle.

Do I need an appointment?

Yes. We do not yet have a storefront so please contact us and arrange a time to drop off your devices.

How much does a repair cost?

Common repairs start at $20 for stick drift, $40 for liquid metal replacement, and $80 for HDMI port repair. Other repairs are quoted on request — just reach out and describe the issue. Diagnostic is always free, so you'll know the cost before any work begins.

Can you diagnose my issue before I commit?

Absolutely. We offer a free diagnostic on every device. We'll identify the problem, explain what's needed, and give you the repair cost — all before any work starts. There's no pressure to proceed if you'd rather not.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and major digital payment methods. Ask us when scheduling and we'll confirm what works best for you.

Is the service local only, or can I ship my device?

We primarily serve the Twin Ports region — Duluth, Superior, and surrounding communities. If you're outside the area, reach out to us and we can discuss shipping arrangements on a case-by-case basis.

What if the same issue comes back after the repair?

All repairs are covered by our 30-day guarantee. If the same issue returns within that window, bring the device back and we'll re-examine it at no charge. We'll either fix it again or provide a refund — whichever makes it right.

Do you work on PS5 controllers and consoles both?

Yes — we repair both DualSense controllers and PS5 consoles. Whether you're dealing with stick drift on a controller or a more serious console fault, we've got you covered.
